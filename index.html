All About Direct Hiring

What is direct hiring and how does it work?

Direct hiring is a process where a foreign employer directly hires a Filipino worker without going through a recruitment agency. The employer must be accredited by the DMW, and the worker's documents will be processed directly through the Department.

What are the requirements for direct hiring?

Requirements include: valid passport, employment contract authenticated by the Philippine Embassy/Consulate, employer's accreditation certificate, worker's credentials and certificates, medical examination results, and other country-specific requirements.

How long does the direct hiring process take?

The processing time varies depending on the destination country and completeness of documents. On average, it takes 2-4 weeks after submission of complete requirements.

DMW E-Registration

How do I register for an E-Registration account?

Visit the DMW E-Registration portal and click "Create Account." You'll need to provide your personal information, valid ID, and contact details. A verification email will be sent to complete your registration.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

Deployment and Records

How can I request a copy of my employment records?

Log in to your E-Registration account and navigate to the "Records" section. You can request and download certified copies of your employment history, contracts, and other deployment documents.
